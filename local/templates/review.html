Always on: Enter approve & print · Shift+Enter approve, no print · Esc close.

This review portal stores data in this browser in localStorage:

  • The mission you last reviewed.
  • The fund period you last set.
  • Recently approved and deleted transactions, so History can reverse them (up to 50 of each).

Approved transactions are written to the local working fund database and a CSV backup. CSV Backups are automatically printed after 100 lines and then cleared to save space. Receipt images are not kept.

Delete transaction

This permanently removes it from the cloud, including its receipts and signature. You can reverse it from History while this app stays open.
