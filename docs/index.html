Local storage on this device

This portal works offline and keeps everything in this browser, on this phone only. Nothing is shared between devices:

  • Mission: the fund you last chose.
  • Wave balance: the latest balance for each mission.
  • Outbox: transactions saved while offline (with their receipt photos) wait here until they sync, then they are removed.
  • Recent: the last 10 transactions per mission, shown above.
  • Autofill: details from past entries to speed up typing (no photos).
  • Presets: your quick fill buttons.
  • Default signatures: a saved signature per name, used when the beneficiary matches and none was drawn.

Clearing this browser's site data, or the Clear buttons in the app, removes it. Once a transaction syncs, it lives on the server.
